## Character Width Analysis

| Badge Type | Current | Option 1 | Option 8 | Width Increase |
|------------|---------|----------|----------|----------------|
| Single | 1 char | 2-3 chars | 2-3 chars | +100-200% |
| With spaces | 3 chars | 5-7 chars | 5-7 chars | +67-133% |

**Impact on line length:**
- 10 traits with single badges: ~150 chars
- 10 traits with complex badges: ~220 chars

**Recommendation:** Still works well on most screens. May wrap on mobile, which is acceptable.

## My Top Recommendation

**Option 8 (Constellation)** because:
1. ✦✧ characters are visually distinctive
2. Celestial theme fits LOT's "navigation" metaphor
3. Progression is clear: ✦· → ✦✧ → ✦✧✦
4. Patterns are unique: ✧·✧ (balance), ✦~✧ (flow), ✧═✧ (consistent)
5. Not too busy, not too simple

**Alternative: Option 1 (Geometric)** if constellation feels too decorative.
